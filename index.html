Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the type of data collected by our Shopify plugin, Aakroline, which is a tool used for implementing branding on merchandise and designing your own models. It also includes how we collect, use, maintain, protect, and disclose this data.

Information Collected Through Shopify's APIs

We collect information provided by Shopify's APIs to enable our plugin to function optimally. This information may include your Shopify store name, the products in your store, the orders placed in your store, and other related business information.

Information Collected Directly from the Merchant

We may also collect personal information directly from the merchant, which may include contact information, such as your email address and phone number. We also generate automated logs related to the usage of our app, such as time spent on the app, features used, and any errors or issues encountered.

Information Collected Directly from Merchants' Customers

In order to provide a seamless and personalized user experience, we might collect some information directly from your customers. We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect this information.
